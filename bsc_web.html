How to make Maggi in Two minuts easily

So, basicly we need some produts to make maggi here are some list of produts

  1. 100ml Water
  2. 1 Maggi
  3. Burner
  4. Container

Main Process

  1. Add 100 ml water to container and turn on burner
  2. Add maggi to container and and massala also
  3. Wait for two to five minuts
  4. Serve hot and enjoy
